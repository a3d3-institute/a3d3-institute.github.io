Multi-messenger Astrophysics

A3D3 is working to integrate AI to promptly and computationally efficiently process the data from telescopes, neutrino detectors, and gravitational-wave detectors in order to quickly identify astronomical events corresponding to the most violent phenomena in the Cosmos. Identification of transient astrophysical phenomena with low latency is a critical element of the larger MMA effort. A3D3 is engaging with the MMA community in order to deepen our understanding of and to unveil previously hidden astrophysical phenomena. This is accomplished thanks to the complementarity of information provided by the gravitational, electromagnetic, weak and strong forces that come into play in such phenomena and the ability to cross-correlate observations using their corresponding messengers.
